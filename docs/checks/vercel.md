# Vercel Checks

- `vercel --prod` - Deploys directly to production environment without staging and prompts for confirmation.

- `vercel remove --yes` - Removes a Vercel project without confirmation and prompts for confirmation.

- `vercel env rm --yes` - Removes environment variables without confirmation and prompts for confirmation.

- `vercel domains rm` - Removes a custom domain from the project and prompts for confirmation.

- `vercel teams rm` - Removes team members from the project and prompts for confirmation.
